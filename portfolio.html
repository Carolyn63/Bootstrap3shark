Our Work

IT Cloud Console

IntrApps incorporates a cloud-based IT management console that includes a range of capabilities and functionality for managing all of your users, devices, apps and cloud services. It provides each employee with their own personalized app, tailored for their devices, which enables instant access to the company's catalog of preferred apps and cloud services. Employees can simply select the apps of their choice, which when approved by you or your IT professional, are then instantly provisioned and deployed, enabling one consistent view of apps and services used across your business. This reduces IT related costs, increases productivity, and has an overall positive effect on the smooth running of your organization.
Analytics and Reporting

Aggregated reporting of all apps and devices used across your business and their associated costs. View reports and charts of app usage, trends, and costs by device and employee.

Company Social Directory

Seamless view of the company, employees, personal profiles, contact details, images, digital and social information.

Company and Personal Catalogues

Detail view of all licensed apps and their availability for use. Search and filtering capabilities enabling viewing by category, type, device, industry and location at both company and personal level.

Procurement, Provisioning and Billing

Placement and tracking of orders for new apps and cloud services. Provision available apps and cloud services across your workforce. View and manage all accounts, cancelled payments, refunds and payment methods.

Management console

Easy-to-use interface for centralized administration and management of employees, companies, profiles, requests, devices, apps, payments, and orders.

Business Social Directory

Organization of your business information as it relates to your key vendors, suppliers, partners, consultants, and business affiliates; including key contact info and locations.
